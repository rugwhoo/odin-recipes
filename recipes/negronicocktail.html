Negroni Cocktail

Description

The Negroni cocktail is a timeless symphony of bold flavors, perfectly balancing bitter, sweet, and aromatic notes. A vivid crimson hue hints at its complexity, with the initial bite of Campari awakening the palate. This bitterness melds seamlessly with the sweet richness of vermouth and the dry, botanical whisper of gin, creating a harmonious medley that lingers beautifully. Served over ice and garnished with a fragrant twist of orange peel, its inviting aroma is as alluring as the cocktail itself.

Each sip unveils a new layer, from the velvety warmth of the fortified vermouth to the crisp zest of citrus that dances on the finish. The Negroni is a drink of sophistication and intrigue, equally suited for contemplative evenings or lively gatherings. Its bold character and classic elegance make it not just a cocktail, but an experience—one that rewards the senses with every swirl of the glass.

Ingredients

  • ¾ fluid ounce campari
  • ¾ fluid ounce gin
  • ¾ fluid ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 fluid ounces carbonated water
  • 1 wedge or peel of orange, for garnish
  • ice

Steps

  1. In a rocks glass over ice, combine Campari, gin, and sweet vermouth. Add carbonated water and garnish with lemon wedge.

Enjoy!
